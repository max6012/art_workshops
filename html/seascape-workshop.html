Seascape Painting Workshop

Capture the magic of light on water

Immerse yourself in the beauty of coastal scenes in this hands-on workshop. Learn to paint luminous skies, reflective water, and atmospheric effects that bring seascapes to life. Whether you prefer serene sunsets or dynamic wave action, you'll develop the skills to capture these moments on canvas.

What You'll Learn

Master the techniques of painting water reflections, dramatic skies, and coastal atmospherics. We'll explore color theory for ocean scenes, learn to suggest movement in waves, and practice creating depth through aerial perspective. Each session includes live demonstrations and personalized feedback.

What to Bring

  • Oil paints (suggested palette: titanium white, cadmium yellow light, cadmium orange, cadmium red, alizarin crimson, ultramarine blue, cerulean blue, viridian)
  • Brushes: variety of filberts and flats, sizes 2-12, plus a large brush for skies
  • Canvas or canvas panels (12x16" or 16x20" recommended for landscapes)
  • Palette, palette knife, and odorless mineral spirits
  • Reference photos (optional - references will be provided)
  • Apron or old clothes
  • Lunch (coffee and snacks provided)

Investment: $185 per session

Class size limited to 12 students for personalized instruction.
