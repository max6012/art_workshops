Seascape Painting Workshop

Saturday, March 7, 2026

During this one-day painting workshop you will learn techniques that will bring out the vibrancy of St Thomas seascapes in your paintings. All levels are welcome!

Example Works

Workshop Schedule

Saturday, March 7

9:00 – 9:30 Welcome & Setup — Introduction to materials and workshop overview
9:30 – 10:30 Master Works Study — Analyzing seascapes from Baroque to contemporary artists
10:30 – 12:00 Quick Color Sketches — Small studies from provided reference images
12:00 – 12:45 Lunch Break
12:45 – 2:30 Painting Session — Work from your own reference to create a St Thomas seascape
2:30 – 3:00 Group Reflection & Critique

What to Bring

  • A reference photo of a seascape you'd like to paint (printed or on your phone/iPad)
  • Your own lunch and plenty of water
  • An apron or clothes you don't mind getting paint on
  • A roll of paper towels
  • Feel free to bring your favorite brushes and paints (optional)

All supplies will be provided.

Workshop Cost: $75
