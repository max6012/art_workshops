Portrait Painting Workshop

Master the art of capturing the human form in oil

Join me for an immersive portrait painting experience in my bright, welcoming studio. This workshop is designed for painters of all levels who want to deepen their understanding of portraiture, from capturing accurate proportions to conveying emotion through brushwork and color temperature.

What You'll Learn

We'll cover essential techniques including color mixing for skin tones, understanding facial structure, working with a live model, and developing your personal approach to portraiture. Each session includes demonstrations, individual guidance, and plenty of painting time.

What to Bring

  • Oil paints (limited palette: titanium white, cadmium yellow, cadmium red, alizarin crimson, ultramarine blue, burnt umber)
  • Brushes: variety of filberts and flats, sizes 2-10
  • Canvas or canvas panels (11x14" or 12x16" recommended)
  • Palette, palette knife, and odorless mineral spirits
  • Apron or old clothes
  • Lunch (coffee and snacks provided)

Investment: $185 per session

Class size limited to 12 students for personalized instruction.
